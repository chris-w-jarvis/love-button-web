What's Stellar Lumens?

One lumen (XLM) is a unit of digital currency, like a bitcoin. Lumens are the native asset of the Stellar network. Native means that lumens are built into the network. Asset is how the network refers to an item of value that is stored on the ledger. One lumen is a unit of digital currency, like a bitcoin. While you can’t hold a lumen in your hand, they are essential to the Stellar network—they contribute to the ability to move money around the world and to conduct transactions between different currencies quickly and securely.

I need a wallet for my Stellar Lumens.

This part is pretty easy! You will want to choose a wallet to store the Stellar Lumens you recieve on love-button. Wallets Recommended by Stellar.

I personally have been happy with the LOBSTR wallet on my phone and the web.

I have my wallet for my Steller Lumens, how do I use love-button?

Whichever wallet you choose will have an option to recieve payment to the wallet. This will expose your public "Stellar address" that love-button will use to send money to you. Make sure you choose Stellar address not federation address. Your Stellar address should be a 56 character string of alphanumeric characters not anything that looks like a url. You will copy this address and paste it into the public key input on this page: Get my link.

Now you have your link! Post this anywhere people see your content and they can click it send some love your way. The defaults links will just have a number ("/pages/00001") but if you want a specialized link ("/pages/YOUR_NAME_HERE") you can follow the steps on Premium Links.
