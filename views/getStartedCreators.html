What are Stellar Lumens?

Stellar is global payment network built on the blockchain and powered by a cryptocurrency called Lumens (also abbreviated as XLM). Learn more about the amazing project: stellar.org. We are using Stellar because the ultra-low transaction fees make micro-tipping possible.

I need a wallet for my Stellar Lumens.

This part is pretty easy! You will want to choose a wallet to store the Stellar Lumens you recieve on love-button. Wallets Recommended by Stellar.

I personally have been happy with the LOBSTR wallet on my phone and the web.

I have my wallet for my Steller Lumens, how do I use love-button?

Whichever wallet you choose will have an option to recieve payment to the wallet. This will expose your public "Stellar address" that love-button will use to send money to you. Make sure you choose "Stellar address" not "federation address". Your Stellar address should be a 56 character string of alphanumeric characters not anything that looks like a url. You will copy this address and paste it into the public key input on this page: Get my link.

Now you have your link! Post this anywhere people see your content and they can click it send some love your way. The default links will just have a number ("/pages/00001") but if you want a specialized link ("/pages/YOUR_NAME_HERE") you can follow the steps on Premium Links.
