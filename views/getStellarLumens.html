Get Stellar Lumens

Getting cryptocurrency can be a bit of a pain the first time but its cool after that I promise!

There are two main options you can take. The first is cheaper but takes longer and consists of buying Ethereum on Coinbase and then transferring your Ethereum to another crypto exchange, such as Binance, where you can buy Stellar Lumens with Ethereum. This process is described here: Lumenauts In this article, when they refer to GDAX they mean Coinbase, the name changed. If you buy with your credit card you can get Ethereum in a few minutes, but the fee is higher, if you use your bank account the fee is lower but you have to wait a few days.

The second option is faster but more expensive at small transaction sizes and consists of buying Stellar directly using StellarX marketplace (under the hood you are buying a stablecoin called AnchorUSD then trading that). This is generally easier but their fee is 1% of transaction and 5$ minimum, so you have to buy a lot more for it be cheaper than Coinbase. This method has become very popular in the Stellar community. Also you have to wait a few days for your bank transfer, do that here: StellarX.

However you go about getting your Stellar Lumens, I highly recommend creating two accounts in your wallet, one where you will store all your assets and then another with only a few dollars at a time for tipping. When you're out of tipping money, just move some more over from your main account.
